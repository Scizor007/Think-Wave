Blackbody Spectrum

What is Blackbody Radiation?

A blackbody is an idealized object that absorbs all incident electromagnetic radiation, regardless of frequency or angle. When heated, it emits radiation in a predictable spectrum, known as blackbody radiation.

The emitted spectrum depends on temperature: - Cooler objects emit infrared radiation (invisible to the human eye). - As temperature increases, emission shifts toward visible light (red, yellow, blue, and beyond). - Extremely hot objects emit ultraviolet radiation.
